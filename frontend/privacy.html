Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026.

What we collect

When you create an account we store:

  • Your email address and a bcrypt hash of your password.
  • Learning data — the text you parse, extracted vocabulary and grammar objects, spaced-repetition state (FSRS), and review history.
  • Language preferences — transliteration and script settings you configure.

We do not sell data, run ads, or share your data with third parties except as required to operate the service (e.g. the database host).

Cookies and storage

Mnemosyne stores a JSON Web Token in sessionStorage while you are signed in. sessionStorage is scoped to the current browser tab and is cleared when the tab is closed. No persistent cookies are set.

Error monitoring

When error monitoring is enabled (Sentry), crash reports and stack traces may be sent to Sentry's servers. No personal data beyond IP address and browser user-agent is included in these reports.

Your rights

  • Access — download all your data at any time via Settings → Export data (or GET /users/me/export).
  • Deletion — permanently delete your account and all associated data. Sign in, then click Delete account in the top-right corner, or send a DELETE /users/me request with your token. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Correction — contact us to correct inaccurate account information.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account all rows linked to your user ID are permanently removed from the database within the same request.

Analytics

Mnemosyne records aggregate, non-identifiable session counts (e.g. "a parse was submitted in language es") to improve the service. No text, vocabulary items, or personal details are included. You can opt out at any time:

Sign in to manage this preference.
